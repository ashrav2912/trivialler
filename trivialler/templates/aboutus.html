About Trivialler

Have you ever planned a trip to a particular place but were too busy to plan your itinerary and your to-do lists? Well, fear NO MORE, as Travallier is here to help you. This web application is your own personal itinerary planner and will help you plan your trips so that you get to experience the location without the hassle of planning it weeks in advance. Just search up your location and add your dates and you're good to go. We also have a forum so that other travellers, who have travelled to the same location can share their itineraries for other people to view. ENJOY USING TRAVALLIER
